L

The distant stars that glitter in the sky
Seem fixed in place by some eternal hand;
Yet though they shine with brilliance from on high,
Their light is cold upon the shadowed land.
But you, whose glow outshines the brightest sphere,
Bring warmth as well as radiance to my night;
Your presence drives away the creeping fear,
And fills the darkness with a gentle light.
No star, however constant in its course,
Can match the steadfast truth your heart displays;
For though the heavens move with silent force,
Your love remains the guide of all my days.
So let the stars adorn the midnight blue,
My firmest light is found alone in you.
