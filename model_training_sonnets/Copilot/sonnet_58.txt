LVIII

The falcon soaring high above the plain
Moves swift and sure with purpose in its flight;
It rides the wind with neither fear nor strain,
And trusts the unseen currents of the height.
So too my hope ascends when you are near,
And rises far beyond the reach of doubt;
It leaves behind the weight of earthly fear,
And finds a path no darkness can cast out.
What wings could lift the spirit so sublime?
What wind could bear it to such lofty view?
Your love alone transcends the bounds of time,
And grants my heart the strength to rise anew.
Thus like the falcon soaring in the blue,
My hope takes flight whenever I seek you.
