LX

Like as the wind that blows across the field,
And shakes the heavy grain within the ear,
So doth the time to all our beauty yield,
And brings the winter of the closing year.
But as the seed within the earth is sown,
To wait the coming of the second spring,
So shall the seeds of love that I have known,
A better harvest to my spirit bring.
Though all the flowers of youth do fade and die,
And leave the world to mourn its lost delight,
I look to thee beyond the cloudy sky,
To guide me through the shadow of the night.
    Then let the seasons turn their wheel around,
    In thy sweet love my only peace is found.
