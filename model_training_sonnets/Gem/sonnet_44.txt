XLIV

If leaden sorrow weigh upon my breast,
And heavy thoughts do chain me to the ground,
I seek in thee a place of quiet rest,
Where only peace and harmony are found.
For thou art like the music of the spheres,
That heals the discord of my weary brain;
Thy gentle voice can dry my bitter tears,
And turn to joy the sharpness of my pain.
When discord strikes the chords of my desire,
And all my soul is out of tune with grace,
Thy touch doth set my spirit all on fire,
And every shadow from my heart efface.
    So in thy love my life is made complete,
    And bitter moments turn to something sweet.
