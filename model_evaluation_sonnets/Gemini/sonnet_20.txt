XX

Now as the curtain falls upon the stage,
And all the actors take their final bow,
I leave this testament upon the page,
To tell the world what I have felt but now.
Thy love hath been the anchor of my soul,
The star that led me through the stormy sea,
The only thing that made my spirit whole,
The greatest gift that heaven gave to me.
Let these poor words survive the wreck of time,
To tell the beauty of thy gentle grace,
And live within the music of my rhyme,
Until the world shall find a better place.
For though I perish and these lines decay,
True love shall never, never pass away.
